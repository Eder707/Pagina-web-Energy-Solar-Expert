Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? Here are the most common ones answered.

Do I need prior sales experience to join?

No. The program is designed for both beginners and experienced salespeople who want to scale their results. We start from the fundamentals and progress to advanced closing strategies.

Does the program include government subsidies?

No. This program is a personal investment in your career and business. We do not offer or manage government subsidies. We teach you to sell solar panels in a professional and ethical way.

How many hours per week do I need to dedicate?

We recommend dedicating 10 to 15 hours per week for the best results: live classes (Monday, Wednesday, and Friday), your 1:1 mentorship session, and field practice. The more time you invest, the faster you'll see results.

What happens when my plan ends (1, 3, or 6 months)?

When your mentorship plan ends, you keep 12 full months of access to all platforms, the CRM, and Knocking Doors University. Only the weekly 1:1 coaching sessions are limited to the duration of your selected plan.

Can I sell solar panels in any state?

The Powur Enterprises platform we work with covers multiple U.S. states. During the application process we will inform you about availability in your specific area.

How do the 1:1 mentorship sessions work?

They are weekly 1-hour Zoom sessions with Luis Bono or one of his certified mentors. We review your progress, address specific obstacles, and define your action plan for the following week. Sessions are completely personalized to your situation.

How do I enroll and when do I start?

Complete the application form on this page. The team will review your request and contact you within 48 hours to confirm your spot and coordinate access to the platforms. Spots are limited — apply today to secure your place.
